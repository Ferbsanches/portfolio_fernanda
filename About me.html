Some more About me:

I am from Brazil, and I am now a Junior Mechanical Engineering student at the University of South Florida (USF) in Tampa.

My greatest passion is turning ideas into reality, which fuels my love for manufacturing, 3D printing, designing, and prototyping. Recently, I have developed a keen interest in project management and am eager to explore this field further.

Throughout my college years, I have remained actively engaged, seeking meaningful and practical experiences.

I currently serve as the Technical Development Director for the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) and am a proud member of the Judy Genshaft Honors College at USF. My professional journey includes an internship at a pharmaceutical company, as well as extensive involvement in various projects where I’ve honed my skills in leading multicultural teams.
